Seedlip Spice Hibiscus Margarita

Description

Made with Seedlip Spice 94 & Agave Syrup, our Hibiscus Margarita strikes a sophisticated balance of sweet & spicy flavours that are complimented by a Sugar-Salt rim & warm Star Anise.

Ingerdients

  • 60ml Seedlip Spice 94
  • 15ml Agave Syrup
  • 30ml Hibiscus Tea
  • 7.5ml Fresh Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Lightly muddle all the ingredients in a shaker.
  2. Add ice and shake and strain over ice.
  3. Garnish with a star anise and a half-rim of spiced sugar/salt.
